What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?
	
	On initializing a repository we tell git to start tracking things in our project. We need to do it in order to initialize git.

How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

	Staging area is the preparation area where the files to be committed . Only files in this area are going to be included in the commit.
